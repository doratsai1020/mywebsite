City Witch


City Witch is a turn-based RPG where players guide Remi, a young witch defending her city from disaster monsters. Featuring a rich narrative, strategic mini-game battles, and a spellbinding urban fantasy setting, I created all the art and crafted the story for this semester-long project in CTIN489.
