Pig & Chikin


Pig & Chikin is a 3D platformer following Pig, a winged pig who loses her ability to fly and must rely on her friend Chikin to adapt. As the only rigger and animator on this USC MFA project, I took on my first 3D artist role in a game, learning extensively about rigging, animation, and collaboration.
